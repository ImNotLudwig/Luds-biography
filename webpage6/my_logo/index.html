My logo

'Shu' is my nickname (irl), it means air. The purple on the bottom represents my favorite colour - purple. The red near the top represents my difference, and how I stand out in my own way. The white that stands out in the front of the image and on the cross beside it represents my simplicity. The cross represents my faith in christ. Few people call me shu, most call me by my full name or my other nicknames. My mom calls me Bob, some of my friends call me Sushi or Why, and I call myself Ludwig.
